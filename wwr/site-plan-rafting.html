Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to inform people about the wonders of white water rafting, how enjoyable it is, and how it is still safe! We encourage people to give it a try, and discover just how fun it can be.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our audience is truly anybody that wants to have a little adventure and a lot of fun! Somebody who has some free time and a little money to spare, and wants to try something new. What do they want? They thirst for adventure, and want to try something new. They are also curious about what white water rafting is and what it entails, and are always willing to try everything at least once. How will my audience access my site? You can access the site via a desktop or laptop primarily, but using a mobile device or tablet should work, also.
